# MedLlama-India-70B: Open-Source LLM for Indian Medical Entrance Exam Preparation

India has one of the world's most competitive postgraduate medical entrance systems. Every year, over **2.3 lakh MBBS graduates** compete for around **70,000 PG seats** through NEET-PG, making quality preparation more important than ever.

## The Problem

For many students, coaching is simply unaffordable. Traditional coaching institutes charge **₹1–2 lakh per year**, while even online courses and QBanks cost thousands of rupees. Students from government medical colleges and lower-income families often have to prepare without expert guidance.

Existing medical AI models also struggle because they are trained mainly on Western medical resources. Indian entrance exams focus on textbooks like **Gray's Anatomy, Robbins, Harrison's, Goodman & Gilman, and Dutta's Obstetrics**, along with Indian disease patterns, national health programmes such as **NTEP, NVBDCP, and NHM**, and Indian clinical practice.

NEET-PG also tests reasoning—not just memorization. Students must understand why the correct answer is right and why every other option is wrong in a specific clinical scenario. Most current open-source medical LLMs predict answers but don't teach this reasoning.

## The Solution

**MedLlama-India-70B** is an open-weight **70B language model** built specifically for Indian postgraduate medical entrance exams.

It is designed around three key pillars:

* **Access:** A free, open-source AI tutor that governments, colleges, NGOs, and startups can deploy to provide affordable, 24×7 exam support.
* **Knowledge:** Fine-tuned on **MedMCQA (194,000+ AIIMS and NEET-PG questions)** covering **21 subjects** and **2,400+ topics**, along with Indian medical guidelines and epidemiology.
* **Reasoning:** Trained to explain not only the correct answer but also why every incorrect option is wrong, helping students build true clinical reasoning instead of rote memorization.

## Impact

MedLlama-India-70B aims to make high-quality PG entrance preparation accessible to every Indian medical student by providing a free, always-available AI tutor that explains concepts, clears doubts, and strengthens exam-solving skills.
